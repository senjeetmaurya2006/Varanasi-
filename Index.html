About Varanasi

Varanasi, one of the world’s oldest living cities, is the spiritual heart of India. From the sacred Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat to the serenity of Sarnath, every corner tells a story. Explorer Varanasi is created by someone deeply connected with the city, sharing authentic experiences and knowledge to make your journey unforgettable.
